About Us

Based in Astana, Kazakhstan, Bon Appetit is a cafe of Heaven Kitchen Group, which is specialized in the restaurant business. The cafe offers different types of desserts alerted according to the season's time.

Owners drew inspiration from European lovely small cafes where people go to take hot beverages with a sweet item during the coffee break or in the morning to start a day with good emotions.

The cafe has a rustic and relaxed atmosphere with moderate prices, making it a popular place for a break at any time of the day.

Bon Appetit was opened in 2019, so it is quite a modern and stylish place, but at the same time calm and peaceful. That is why there are people from different generations comfortably spent time together.
